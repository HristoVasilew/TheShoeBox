About The Shoe-Box

The Shoe-Box Shoes feature clean, The Shoe-Box was founded in Southern California in 1991 as a reaction to the excessive design & overproduced marketing we saw everywhere – a brand that mixed our influences of surfing, skating, art but expressed it honestly. Without hype.

The Shoe-Box fit the new world. People wanted less, and they didn’t want their shoes doing the talking for them. 

This ethos still holds true today. From our shoes to our clothing, our design is considerate and deliberate. We provide clean, stylish products that are responsibly manufactured with recycled materials. 

Things that not only look good, but that are good for the planet. 

As we prepare for the next 30 years, our future is wide open.  But what we know is this:

In a world that's more chaotic than ever, we're proud to be Simple.
